About Us

Symphony, the college's longstanding music club, has been a hub for musical talent since its inception. It's a place where musicians come together to share their love for music, perform at college events, and compete in inter-college competitions.

The club hosts musical nights where anyone can showcase their talents, adding a lively vibe to the campus. Members also enjoy casual jamming sessions in the music room, creating a fun and relaxed atmosphere.
