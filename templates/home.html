IIIT-Blare

Premier technical institute driving innovation

IIITB’s academics emphasize research‑driven, hands‑on learning, offering Integrated and standalone M.Tech, M.Sc. in Digital Society, postgraduate diplomas, B.Tech, MS by Research and Ph.D. across Computer Science, ECE, Data Science, AI, Cybersecurity and Networks. Courses blend theory with industry projects, labs and internships. A flexible credit system and interdisciplinary electives foster innovation, while faculty‑led research ensures cutting‑edge scholarship and mentorship.

Fests

Infin8

Infin8 is an inter-college techno-cultural festival conducted by IIIT-Bangalore every year. Students participate with enthusiasm and compete with other college teams for the podium finish.

Synergy

Synergy, the tech fest of IIIT-Bangalore, ignites minds with innovation and collaboration, fostering a dynamic ecosystem where ideas converge.

Spandan

Spandan, IIIT-Bangalore's annual sports meet held in March, is a vibrant three-day event packed with diverse indoor and outdoor games. Beyond sports, Spandan serves as a nostalgic reunion for alumni, strengthening ties between past and present generations.
